# QManager v2 - Product Overview

QManager v2 is a next-generation Commercial Management platform designed to handle the end-to-end lifecycle of leads, quotations, approvals, and invoicing.

## Core Features

1. **Lead Management**: Track potential sales, map them to sources, and manage the sales funnel.
2. **Quotation Engine**: 
   - Multi-service support (Smart Home, IT, CCTV).
   - Approval workflows (Threshold-based triggers).
   - Revision tracking and version control.
   - Dynamic Terms & Scope merging.
3. **Advanced PDF Generation**:
   - Uses Puppeteer/Next.js for pixel-perfect React-based PDF templates.
   - Separate themes per service type.
4. **Customer Portal**:
   - Token-based secure links for clients.
   - Digital Acceptance with e-signatures.
   - Invoice viewing and payment proof uploads.
5. **Billing & Invoicing**:
   - Convert Quotations to Invoices (Advance, Milestone, Final).
   - Track payments and outstanding balances.
6. **Reporting & Analytics**:
   - Live KPI dashboard.
   - Exportable CSV reports.
